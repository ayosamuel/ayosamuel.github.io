ib vogt GmbH • Solar PV Project Data Management

Global Meteorological Intelligence on Demand

The Challenge

Business Stakeholders: Experience increased costs and investment risks from repeated data purchases and unreliable modeling inputs.
Project Engineers: Face simulation inaccuracies and project delays due to inconsistent, fragmented access to historical weather data.
Data team: Become overwhelmed by manual ingestion tasks and repeated requests, with no scalable or automated workflow in place.

The Build

Implement a centralized, traceable, and efficient process for managing historical meteorological data to support accurate simulations and reduce redundant data requests.

Impact

Avoided cost of redundant purchase of data. Data aquisition workflow reduced by roughly 50%, giving engineers self-service confidence and ensuring modelling windows were met without escalation.

ib vogt GmbH • SCADA & Sensor Data Modeling

Solar Performance Pipeline and Analytics

The Challenge

Performance tests reporting was fragmented across managers and regions; site and SCADA data and systems format varied across different providers; analytics were manual and slow; stakeholders (asset owners, advisors, EPC, O&M) needed timely, defensible results to hit PAC and KPI targets..

The Build

Designed a modular data model and orchestrated Python ETL pipelines (local and on Azure). Automated validations, harmonized as-built data, and delivered analytics-ready outputs for reporting androot-cause diagnostics.

Impact

Equipped engineers and asset managers with daily performance metrics, enabling faster commissioning tests across ~1.2 GW portfolio and sustained operational excellence.
